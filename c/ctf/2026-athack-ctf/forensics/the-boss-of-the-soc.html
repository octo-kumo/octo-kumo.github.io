sub = df[

At this point I knew that greenshot.exe was actually anydesk and exploit was in action, so I looked for when it was ran to pinpoint the time of the attack.

Then I located it to be between 2026-02-04 02:25:00 and 2026-02-04 03:08:10 (by vibes).

Then I just took all the timestamps, tried all of them by brute force, with ±10\pm10±10 seconds, and got the answer for q5.

  • 2026-02-04 02:28:37

Afterwards I increased the margin to ±100\pm100±100 seconds and got the answer for q6.

  • 2026-02-04 02:27:30
