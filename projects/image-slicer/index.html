Image Slicer

Slice up your images based on grid size, only PNGs are exacto-cropped, other images are auto-converted to PNG via canvas API. Sliced images will be downloaded as a zip file. You can change both grid size and grid item size, they sync automatically. In case of fractional grid, extra parts will be discarded, you can also discard empty grids.

Everything happens in your browser, nothing is uploaded to anywhere.

You may run out of memory if the images are too large!
