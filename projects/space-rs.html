Rust 2D Gravity Sim

space-rs

Space RS is a 2D N-body simulation game written in rust.

Play Online

How to play

  1. Left click, drag and release to create a body.
  2. Right click, to cancel the body creation.
  3. Middle drag to pan the camera.
  4. Scroll to zoom in and out.
