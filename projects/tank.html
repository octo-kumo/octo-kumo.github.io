The game

A simple version of tank side shooter with following features.

  • Level selection
  • Ammo selection
    • Many types of ammo! + special hidden ammo types!
    • Beautiful effects!
  • Fuel barrels for strategic maneuvers
  • Bomber planes
  • PVP
